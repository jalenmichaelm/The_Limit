What is The Limit

The Limit is a single purpose app designed to help make your driving experince easier. It provides you the speed limit of the road you are on anywhere.

Quick

The Limit brings up the information you need in matter of seconds. And Updates in the background whenever you change speed zones.

Easy

All you have to do is click and go. The Limit is designed to keep you off your phone as much as possible but still give you the information you need to get going.
